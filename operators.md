| --- | --- | --- |

| `-` | Subtraction | `int` - `int` |
| `*` | Multiplication | `int` * `int` |
| `/` | Division | `int` / `int` |
| `%` | Modulo | `int` % `int` |

| `-.` | Subtraction | `float` - `float` |
| `*.` | Multiplication | `float` * `float` |
